About Us

The evolution and spread and antibiotic resistance in pathogenic bacteria poses an alarming threat to human life in its potential to make obsolete that which is arguably the greatest medical achievement of the last century: the discovery and development of antibiotics. Our team seeks to contribute to the mitigation of this threat by determining the most effective mechanisms by which CRISPR-Cas9 systems may be inserted into antibiotic resistant bacteria.

Our Research Question

What are the most effective, safe, and applicable methods by which antibiotic-resistant microbes may be systematically targeted and eliminated in the context of the human extracellular environment?
